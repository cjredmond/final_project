Rules

  1. Teams consist of 3 Football, Soccer, Basketball, and Hockey pro teams
  2. You will play one person in your league each week starting and ending Tuesday midnight
  3. When a team you own starts a game, you will be locked with their score
  4. If you drop a team during a game, you will still be given their score
  5. Scoring is different for each sport but is calculated using 3 categories: win/loss, point differential, points scored
